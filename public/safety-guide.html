Safety Guide

Practical safety tips for rides on SahaVahan. Last updated: 10-Jun-2026

Your safety matters. This guide provides recommended practices for both drivers and passengers. If you ever feel unsafe, you should end the ride and contact local emergency services.

1. Before You Start

  • Verify identities: use verification features (OTP/KYC) provided by the platform.
  • Confirm details: double-check route, pickup point, and timing shown in the app.
  • Meet in safe locations: choose well-lit and public pickup/drop areas.
  • Check vehicle and registration: ensure the vehicle matches ride details before entering.

2. During the Ride

  • Share ride status: keep the app open when possible so updates can be sent.
  • Stay alert: avoid distractions and do not ignore safety concerns.
  • Respect comfort boundaries: do not engage in harassment or unsafe behavior.
  • Follow road rules: seat belts (where applicable), safe driving, and appropriate speed.

3. Communication & Conduct

  • Communicate through in-app tools when available.
  • Do not share sensitive personal information that is not needed for the ride.
  • Report suspicious messages or behavior using available report features.

4. Reporting Issues

If something goes wrong, report it promptly. Accurate reports help us take action and improve safety.

5. Emergency

In an emergency, contact local emergency services immediately. Do not wait for platform support.

6. Our Commitment

SahaVahan uses verification and trust mechanisms designed to reduce risk, but no platform can eliminate all safety threats. We encourage every user to follow safe practices and remain vigilant.
