Privacy Policy

How SahaVahan (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information. Last updated: 10-Jun-2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal data when you use SahaVahan’s carpooling platform, including booking and publishing rides.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, username, email/phone (if provided), and password-related data (we store password hashes, not plain passwords).
  • Profile and verification: profile details, verification status, and trust-related information.
  • Ride data: ride requests, routes, pickup/drop details, timing, and booking history.
  • Communication: messages exchanged between users (where applicable).
  • Device & usage: IP address, browser/device information, and general usage analytics (e.g., pages visited).
  • Location data: approximate or precise location data you provide or that is needed to coordinate rides (e.g., pickup/drop points).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the platform (matching, booking, managing rides).
  • To verify users and maintain trust and safety (e.g., KYC/OTP flows where used).
  • To communicate with you about rides, safety notifications, and account updates.
  • To improve features, reliability, and user experience.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or other security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share information in limited circumstances, such as:

  • With other users: certain ride details (e.g., route, pickup/drop points) are shown to coordinate travel.
  • With service providers: vendors that help us run the platform (e.g., hosting, messaging, analytics).
  • Legal and safety: if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may update your profile information and verification status (where available).
  • You may request access to or deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational requirements.
  • You may opt out of certain non-essential communications where applicable.

7. Children’s Privacy

SahaVahan is intended for users who can legally use our services. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. International Transfers

If you access the platform from outside India (or where we operate), your data may be processed in different jurisdictions.
